About Me

I'm Jener ARAP, born in November 2006, and raised in Prizren, Kosovo. I have a deep passion for robotics and programming, dedicating myself to advancing in this field and contributing to the progress of my country. I have been involved in robotics since the age of 10 and have always been immensely curious about this domain. You can explore my robotic projects, skills, and work experience as evidence of my dedication. I have exerted considerable effort to progress in this field, attending technology schools, participating in competitions, and engaging in various robotic projects to gain more experience. Additionally, I worked in the textile industry to cover the expenses of attending technology school, purchasing necessary materials, electronic devices, and computers. Currently, starting from February 20, 2024, I will be teaching robotics at the Yunus Emre Institute Prizren center, where I have my workshop. Previously, I conducted a course on 3D design at the Yunus Emre Institute under the "Genç Komek" program. Stay tuned, as I will soon realize my biggest dream, which will be a first of its kind project in Kosovo. I plan to continue my university life in Turkey in order to receive a higher quality education and reach advanced levels. Keep following me.
